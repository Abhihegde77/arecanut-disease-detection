What are Arecanut Diseases?

Arecanut diseases are various ailments that affect the arecanut palm (Areca catechu), also known as betel nut. These diseases can significantly impact the health and productivity of arecanut plantations. Common diseases include yellow leaf disease, foot rot, and inflorescence dieback.
